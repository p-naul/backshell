Help

    [1] Add a case and customize
    • Click on a case on left table, it appears in center
    • Change its height with the sliders
    [2] Add a chimney to your customization
    • Click on a chimney on right table, it appears on the sphere
    • Click on a button on sphere to change its orientation
    • Click on the surface of the case, chimney moves to this position
    • Change its position selecting another area on the case
    Then
  • Repeat step [2] to add more chimneys
  • To modify the orientation of a chimney after it's placed on case, select it on case, it appears on the sphere, then change its orientation and click on case to transfer it back
